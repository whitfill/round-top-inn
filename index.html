Round Top Inn

Relax in our Historic Inn

Round Top Inn welcomes you to the heart of the Texas countryside. Relax, put your boots up and stay awhile! We're located in downtown Round Top, a tiny community known as one of the most charming and vibrant small towns in Texas.

Our property features thirteen beautifully restored guestrooms across seven vintage cottages set on manicured grounds framed by majestic oaks. Each guest room is decorated in a style we call sophisticated country, blending historic charm with modern comforts.

Enjoy luxurious linens, a full hot breakfast featuring locally sourced ingredients, complimentary Wi-Fi, and access to our Dining Room and Segar House Lounge. Unique boutiques, galleries, and restaurants are just a short stroll away.

About Round Top & the Antique Shows

Round Top hosts major antique shows twice a year: spring (late March through early April) and fall (late September through early October). A smaller winter show takes place in January. Each show runs approximately 10–14 days across 48+ venues. Visit Round Top Finder for the complete 2026 schedule with exact dates.

Round Top offers year-round charm: unique boutiques, art galleries, the renowned Festival Hill concert series, farm-to-table restaurants, wineries, and beautiful countryside drives. Many shops and venues are open year-round, not just during show weeks. Browse things to do in Round Top for a complete guide.

Warrenton is a neighboring town about 5 miles from Round Top that hosts antique shows on the same schedule. Warrenton is known for large open-air tent shows and flea market-style bargains, while Round Top venues tend to feature more curated antiques. Most visitors shop both towns during a single trip. See all Round Top & Warrenton antique shows on Round Top Finder.

Round Top has wonderful dining options, from casual Texas BBQ to elevated farm-to-table experiences. Royers Round Top Cafe is a local favorite, and several restaurants are within walking distance of the Inn. Browse the full Round Top dining guide to plan your meals.
